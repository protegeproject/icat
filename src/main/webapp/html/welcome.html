Welcome to iCAT - the ICD 11 Collaborative Authoring Tool!

This platform is used for the authoring of the ICD 11 alpha draft.

To edit the content of ICD 11 categories, please go to the ICD Content tab and select the category in the hierarchy.
To view or create notes and discussions related to a category, please go to the Category Notes and Discussions tab.

 Now you can receive notifications about changes and discussions. Click here to find out how.

For a visual tutorial please take a look at our screen cast series explaining the iCAT functionality.

If you have any questions related to the tool, would like to submit a feature request or bug report, or just give us feedback, submit your question on the icat-users Google Group.

More information about the ICD 11 Revision process can be found on the ICD-11 Revision site.

The iCAT release notes are available on the icat-users Google Group .
